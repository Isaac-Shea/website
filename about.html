About Me

//

I Make Stuff

That's Cool, Right? //

I’m a Brisbane-based, self-taught graphic design and programming enthusiast. I’ve been making content on the internet since 2015, and have always loved the intersection between technical computing and user experience.

I’ve made a game, two Discord bots, multiple websites and various designs, commissions and not. My interests are wide, and I enjoy learning new things. I’m familiar with C#, Python, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

My Story

</>

I started making content on the novice programming site Scratch in 2015 with basic interface examples, but after I became bored of that I challenged myself to develop a game over the Christmas break of 2016/17. From there, I began development of my first (and to date only) game, Gravity,

I continued the development of Gravity for nearly 3 years, finishing it in late 2019 (you can read about the whole story here).

In 2020, I had far too much time on my hands, being stuck at home and all, and decided to take up learning Python with the sole intention of heartlessly insulting my friends (which you can read about here), and found a love for UI & UX design, which I’d like to focus on largely in 2021.

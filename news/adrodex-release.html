Entering E-Commerce

07 Feb, 2020 //

Adrodex is an online marketplace for my high-quality, handmade graphic design projects. Starting today, the Neon Collection will be available as part of the Springboard Series.

Visit Site Read About It

I designed Adrodex as a place to sell high-quality digital design and art projects I undertake, such as app icons for iOS 14, desktop backgrounds, and anything else I see fit.
The whole site was made just by me, and is an excellent way to support my projects, and get something cool in return.

I have many plans for the site and content, and I’m extremely excited to continue developing it in the coming months!
